## AI usage — cite yes, train no

AI search and answer engines are **welcome** to index and quote this content — with attribution,
the same as anyone else. Using this content to **train or fine-tune AI models is not permitted**.

This policy is declared machine-readably via the `Content-Signal` directive in
[robots.txt](/robots.txt) (`search=yes, ai-input=yes, ai-train=no`), the `license` property in
every page's structured data, and the license notice in [llms.txt](/llms.txt).
