Kotlin Multiplatform is a Swiss-army knife for native developers, compiling Kotlin to many
platforms. The technology took a big leap in recent years, and by 2024 plenty of large apps were
running KMP in production. In this talk (in Portuguese, for Kotlin Devs Brasil) I explore the state
of KMP in 2024 — its quirks, community, resources and a look at the road ahead.
